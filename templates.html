Application Templates:

Applying for SIV is a very lengthy process. There are five steps in the application process. However, the most important step is the first step, we recommend reading the the application instructions here very carefully before applying.

When you read the application instructions, you will notice that beside your personal documents, there are three main documents that you need for your application package. Applicants usually don't know how to prepare these documents. First of all, remember that there is no standard template for these document, as long as they meet the requirements explained in the application instructions, they are okay. However, we have created some templates for the three main documents that are needed for the first step of your application. Please read these templates carefully and follow the instructions. These templates will help you get an idea of how you should prepare your documents. See below to download these docuemnts.
