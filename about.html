About Us:

This website is built by an Afghan SIV and its content is created by a group of Afghan SIVs who live in the US now. We know all the challenges that come with the resettlment process and building your new home here in the US. Our mission is to serve as a resource for our fellow Afghans to help them have a smooth transition to their new life in the US.

Some of the services that we provide to our fellow Afghans are as follow:

  • Help them with the initial application process and procedures.
  • Provide them with the templates of the documents needed for applying to SIV program.
  • Answer their questions about the eligibility requirments of the program.
  • Help them choose the right State in the US for their resettlment.
  • Provide information about life and culture in the US.
  • Help them find resources after they are resettled in the US.

    • We also have a Facebook page with more than 17 thousand members. We post updates and resource to our Facebook page regularly. We also have some videos on our Facebook page that can help SIV applicants with their application package. To visit our Facebook page, please click here.

    Important Note for the Afghan SIV applicants:

    All of our materials and servies are 100% free. Anyone who asks you for any kind of fees or money and claims that they are from our Association, they are not! please don't trust them and don't pay anything to anyone for the information and documents available on this website.
